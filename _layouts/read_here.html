how to get your following.html and followers.html

This tutorial was created in March 2025. If there are any changes, please contact me.

  1. Open Settings > Account Center
  2. Click Download Your Information
  3. Click Download and Transfer Information
  4. Click Some of Your Information
  5. Check Followers and Following
  6. Click Download to Device
  7. Click Date Range
  8. Select All Time
  9. Done! Wait for Instagram to generate the file.
  10. This files from Instagram | connections > followers_and_following | Upload your files followers and following
